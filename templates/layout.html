How it works

Our new Product allows you to upload a picture of your medical report to assess and extract personal and medical information, and displaying the data in form of a table, easing the burden of the medical staff and patients to find the required information.


SNAP

Take a picture of your medical report. For best results, try to focus on the entire page and contain most of the report in the picture.

SUBMIT

Upload your picture onto our Product using our handy web app and image upload capabilities.

ASSESS

This product will identify and extract text and immediately the data in the form of tables and graphs.

RESULTS

Results can be used to interpret the report easily and also find necessary information during hospitalization/discharge.
